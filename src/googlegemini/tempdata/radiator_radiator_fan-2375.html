Connect the Coolant Hose to the Connector Coupling

  Note

Replacing the damaged clip -4-.

- Remove the old O-ring -2- in the coolant hose -3-.

- Coat the new O-ring with coolant and insert it in the coolant hose.

- Press the coolant hose on to the coolant pipe -1- until it engages audibly.

- Press the coolant hose on again and pull to make sure the connector coupling is engaged correctly.
