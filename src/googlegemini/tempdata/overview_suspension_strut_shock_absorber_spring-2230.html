Coil Spring and Spring Plate Installation Position

  • The end of the spring -1- must be positioned to the stop on the spring plates -2--arrow-.
  • The positioning pins of the spring plate must engage at the top in the holes in the body -3- and at the bottom in the holes in the rear transverse link -4- as shown.

Shock Absorber Mount, Removing and Installing

  • Shock absorber is removed.

Equipment Versions with Electronic Damping:

- Free up the wire on the cover.

- Unlock the contacts and remove the connector housing from the wire. Refer to → Electrical Equipment General Information; Rep. Gr.97; Unlocking and Disassembling from Connector Housings.

- Remove the wiring guide from the nut.

Continuation for All Versions

- Arrange the special tools as shown.
