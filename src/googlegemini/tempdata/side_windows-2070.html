Overview - Rear Side Window

Overview - Rear Side Window, Sedan

1 -  Rear Side Window

  • Removing and installing. Refer to → Chapter "Rear Side Window, Removing and Installing".
  • After installing, push in using the locating pins -7-

2 -  Adhesive Bead

  • Observe minimum curing times. Refer to → Chapter "Minimum Curing Times for Bonded Windows".
  • Different distances to window edge:
  • Dimension -a- = 8 mm
  • Dimension -b- = 7.5 mm
  • Dimension -c- = 7.5 mm
  • Adhesive bead thickness.

3 -  Side Window Frame

4 -  Molding

  • Must not be damaged in the visible area

5 -  Trim Molding

  • Only for versions with the gloss package

6 -  Securing Hole

7 -  Centering Pin

  • No replacement part
  • Is cut through when removing the window
