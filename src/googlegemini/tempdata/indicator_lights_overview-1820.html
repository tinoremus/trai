Audi A4: Indicator lights overview

Audi A4 (B9) 2016-2023 Owner's Manual / Summary / Indicator lights overview

Description

The indicator lights in the instrument cluster blink or turn on. They indicate functions or malfunctions.

Some warning and indicator lights turn on when you switch the ignition on and must turn off when the drive system is switched on or while driving.

With some indicator lights, messages may appear and warning signals may sound. The indicator lights and messages may be covered by other displays. To show them again, select the second tab for messages with the multifunction steering wheel.

Some indicator lights in the display can display in several colors.

Central indicator light

If the or indicator light turns on, check the message in the instrument cluster.
