Audi A4: ATF

Audi A4 (B9) 2016-2023 Service Manual / Transmission / 7-Speed Dual Clutch Transmission 0CJ, 0CK, 0CL / Controls, Housing / ATF

ATF Level, Checking

Special tools and workshop equipment required

  • Vehicle Diagnostic Tester
  • Used Oil Collection and Extraction Unit -SMN372500-
  • Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler Kit -VAS6617- with Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler - Hand Pump -VAS6617/1- and Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler - Adapter 2 -VAS6617/2-
  • Adapter -VAS6617/12-
  • Protective Eyewear
  • Acid-Resistant Safety Gloves

Test Conditions

  • The transmission must not be emergency running
  • Move the vehicle onto a 4-column workshop hoist or over a work pit so it is completely level.
  • Transmission is in "P".
  • The parking brake button is pressed to activate the electro-mechanical parking brake.
  • Exhaust hose/-hoses of an exhaust extraction system connected.
  • The A/C system and the heater are off.
  • Vehicle Diagnostic Tester is connected.
  • The ATF temperature must not be higher than 35 ºC (95 ºF) at the beginning of the test. Let the transmission must cool down first, if necessary.

  Note

  • The ATF level will vary depending on the temperature of the ATF.
  • It is possible to overfill with ATF if the level is checked when the temperature is too low.
  • It is possible to underfill with ATF if the level is checked when the temperature is too high.
  • Both overfilling and underfilling impair the function of the transmission.

Procedure

  Note

Replace the plug for the inspection and fill hole.

- Connect the Vehicle Diagnostic Tester with the vehicle and start the Diagnostic mode.

  • Select vehicle test
  • 02-Transmission electronics, functions
  • 02-ATF level ⇒ Check the ATF level

- Follow the instructions in the display.

  Caution

Risk of damaging the transmission.

  • Engine must not be started if only a small amount or no ATF is in the transmission after repair work or after significant ATF loss.
  • In this case, pre-filling is necessary. Refer to → Chapter "ATF, Draining and Filling".

- Remove the rear noise insulation. Refer to → Body Exterior; Rep. Gr.66; Noise Insulation; Noise Insulation, Removing and Installing.

  Note

  • The ATF level is checked at the ATF check and fill hole.
  • The ATF level is correct if a small amount of fluid drips out of the inspection and fill opening (this is normal as the fluid expands as it heats up) at an ATF temperature between 35 º and 45 ºC (95 to 113 ºF).

- Place the Used Oil Collection and Extraction Unit -SMN372500- underneath.

  WARNING

Danger of eye injury.

Wear protective eyewear.

- As soon as an ATF temperature of 35 ºC (95 to 113 ºF) is reached, remove the plug -arrow- on the inspection and fill hole or open the plugs.

  Note

An aluminum oil pan is shown.

- If necessary let any excess ATF run out.

  Note

If the ATF starts to drip out of the inspection and fill hole before it reaches 45 ºC (113 ºF), then the ATF level is OK.

- Assemble the vehicle.

If no ATF drips out of the inspection and fill hole before it reaches 45 ºC (113 ºF), fill with ATF:

- Switch off the engine and let the transmission cool down to 35 ºC (95 ºF).

  Caution

Risk of damaging the transmission.

  • Only use the listed replacement ATF for the dual-clutch transmission 0CJ, 0CK, 0CL.
  • Using other fluids can cause malfunctions or transmission failure.
  • Note the allocation. Refer to the Parts Catalog.
  • The Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler Kit -VAS6617- must be clean and ATF must not be mixed with another fluid! Rinse the hand pump with ATF if necessary.

  WARNING

Danger of eye injury.

Wear protective eyewear.

- Connect the Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler Kit -VAS6617- with Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler - Hand Pump -VAS6617/1- and Pressurized Gearbox Oil FIller - Adapter 2 -VAS6617/12- to the ATF check and fill hole.

  Caution

Risk of damaging the transmission by mixing ATF and transmission fluid (MTF) through the same bleeder.

The ATF filling quantity of 0.5L must not be exceeded.

- Fill the transmission with 0.5L of ATF using the hand pump.

- Repeat the "Check ATF level" procedure in its entirety.

Assembling

  Note

The ATF check and fill hole must be sealed off again before the ATF reaches 45 ℃ (113 ºF).

- Tighten the plug on the inspection and fill hole or close the plugs all the way. The ATF level test is now completed.

Tightening Specifications

  • Plug: Refer to -item 1-.
  • Refer to → Body Exterior; Rep. Gr.66; Noise Insulation; Overview - Noise Insulation.

ATF, Draining and Filling

Special tools and workshop equipment required

  • Vehicle Diagnostic Tester
  • Used Oil Collection and Extraction Unit -SMN372500-
  • Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler Kit -VAS6617- with Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler - Hand Pump -VAS6617/1- and Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler - Adapter 2 -VAS6617/2-
  • Protective Eyewear
  • Acid-Resistant Safety Gloves

Draining

  Note

Replace the plugs after removal.

  • The engine is off.
  • Move vehicle onto a four-pillar workshop hoist or over a work pit so that it is absolutely horizontal.
  • The selector level is in "P".
  • The parking brake button is pressed to activate the electro-mechanical parking brake.

- Remove the rear noise insulation. Refer to → Body Exterior; Rep. Gr.66; Noise Insulation; Noise Insulation, Removing and Installing.

- Place the Used Oil Collection and Extraction Unit -SMN372500- underneath.

- Connect the Vehicle Diagnostic Tester with the vehicle and start the Diagnostic mode.

  • Select vehicle test
  • 02-Transmission electronics, functions
  • 02-ATF level ⇒ Drain ATF

- Follow the instructions in the display.

  WARNING

Danger of eye injury.

Wear protective eyewear.

- Remove the plug -arrow- on the drain opening or open the plugs and let the ATF drain.

  Caution

Risk of damaging the transmission.

Do not start the engine when there is no ATF in the transmission.

  Note

Follow the disposal requirements.

- Tighten the ATF drain plug.

- Clean the subframe crossbrace and remove any remaining ATF.

Filling

  Caution

Risk of damaging the transmission.

  • Only use the listed replacement ATF for the dual-clutch transmission 0CJ, 0CK, 0CL.
  • Using other fluids can cause malfunctions or transmission failure.
  • Note the allocation. Refer to the Parts Catalog.
  • The Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler Kit -VAS6617- must be clean and ATF must not be mixed with another fluid! Rinse the hand pump with ATF if necessary.

  WARNING

Danger of eye injury.

Wear protective eyewear.

- Remove the plug -arrow- on the inspection and fill hole or open the plugs all the way.

- Connect the Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler Kit -VAS6617- with Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler - Hand Pump -VAS6617/1- and Pressurized Gearbox Oil Filler - Adapter 2 -VAS6617/12- to the ATF check and fill hole.

  Caution

Risk of damaging the transmission by mixing ATF and transmission fluid (MTF) through the same bleeder.

The ATF filling quantity of 3.5 liters (3.7 quarts) must not be exceeded.

- Fill the transmission with 3.5 (3.7 quarts) liters of ATF using the hand pump.

- Connect the Vehicle Diagnostic Tester with the vehicle and start the Diagnostic mode.

  • Select vehicle test
  • 02-Transmission electronics, functions
  • 02-ATF level ⇒ 02-Fill ATF

- Start the selected program and follow the instructions in the display of the Vehicle Diagnostic Tester.

- Tighten the plug -arrow- on the inspection and fill hole or close the plugs all the way.

Tightening Specifications
