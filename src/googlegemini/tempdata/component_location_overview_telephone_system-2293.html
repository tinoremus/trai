Overview - Microphone Unit

One microphone (Interior Microphone -R74-) is connected directly to the Digital Sound System Control Module -J525-. The other microphones are connected to the Information Electronics Control Module 1 -J794-.

For IW3 the Left Front Microphone -R140- is connected with the Data Bus on Board Diagnostic Interface -J533- and for 9ZE are "looped through" on the Information Electronics Control Module 1 -J794-.
