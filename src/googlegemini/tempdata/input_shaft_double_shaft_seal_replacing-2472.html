Audi A4: Input Shaft Double Shaft Seal, Replacing

Audi A4 (B9) 2016-2023 Service Manual / Transmission / Servicing - 7-Speed Dual Clutch Transmission 0CJ, 0CL, 0CK / Clutch / Input Shaft Double Shaft Seal, Replacing

Special tools and workshop equipment required

  • Puller - Crankshaft/Power Steering Seal -T20143-
  • Thrust Piece -T40300-
  • Clutch, engaging bearing and slave cylinder removed. Refer to → Chapter "Dual Clutch, Removing and Installing".

- With the Puller - Crankshaft/Power Steering Seal -T20143- carefully remove the seal -arrow- from the housing.

- A waved washer -arrow- is located behind the seal, it does not need to be replaced.

  Note

Pay attention to the installed position.

- Drive in the new seal to the stop using the Thrust Piece -T40300-.

- Install the clutch. Refer to → Chapter "Dual Clutch, Removing and Installing".

Input Shaft Seal, Replacing

Special tools and workshop equipment required

  • Puller - Crankshaft/Power Steering Seal -T20143-
  • Assembly Sleeve -T40308-
  • Thrust Piece -T50038-

Removing

  • The clutch is installed.

  Caution

There is a risk of damaging the clutch from mishandling.

The shaft seal can only be replaced when the clutch is installed. Refer to → Anchor

- Pry out the shaft seal -arrow- carefully with the Puller - Crankshaft/Power Steering Seal -T20143-.

  Note

Do not damage the contact surface on the shaft.

Installing

- Place the Assembly Sleeve -T40308- on the drive axle.

- Drive in the new seal with the Thrust Piece -T50038- in the clutch housing until it stops.

- Remove the Assembly Sleeve -T40308-.
