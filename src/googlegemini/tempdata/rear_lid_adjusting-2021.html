  • Adjustment dimensions. Refer to → Chapter "Adjustment Dimensions".

Procedure

- Remove the lock carrier trim panel. Refer to → Body Interior; Rep. Gr.70; Luggage Compartment Trim Panels; Lock Carrier Trim Panel, Removing and Installing.

- Before adjusting, install the lid-side adjusting buffer entirely. Refer to → Chapter "Height, Adjusting using Adjusting Buffer".

- Loosen the nuts -1 and 3-. The striker -2- must be movable.

- Close the lid and check the closing position.

- If necessary, adjust the closing position using the upper adjusting buffer.

- The rear lid latch must be engaged in the main detent (second catch) at the same time.

- Open the lid and tighten the nuts.

- Adjust the adjusting buffer. Refer to → Chapter "Height, Adjusting using Adjusting Buffer".

Tightening Specifications

  • Refer to → Chapter "Overview - Rear Lid"

Height, Adjusting using Adjusting Buffer

  • Adjustment dimensions. Refer to → Chapter "Adjustment Dimensions".

Procedure

- Install the adjusting screw -2- with the protective cap -1- all the way into the adjusting buffer -3- before adjusting the lid.

- Remove the adjusting screw -2- just enough until the protective cap -1- makes contact with the stop/buffer with light pretension when the rear lid is closed.

- Check if the lid is without play and in flush position by pressing or pulling at the side edges of the rear lid.

- Repeat the adjustment if necessary.
