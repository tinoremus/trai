Towing Recognition Control Module -J345-, Removing and Installing

Removing

- Open the left service cover. Refer to → Body Interior; Rep. Gr.70; Luggage Compartment Trim Panels; Overview - Luggage Compartment Side Trim Panel.

- Disconnect the connectors -4 through 7-.

- Release the retainers -1- in the direction of -arrows- and remove the Towing Recognition Control Module -J345--2- from the bracket -3-.

Installing

Install in reverse order of removal.
